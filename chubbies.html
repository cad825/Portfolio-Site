CHUBBIES

Rowdy Gentleman’s Spring collections were inspired by all things tropical island vacation. For the Spring 19 Collection, I knew I wanted to do a mermaid design, but as a brand, we always strived to inject some level of humor or unexpectedness into our artwork whenever possible. While pondering how I could push a mermaid into the humorous or unexpected realm, I recalled that, in previous seasons, we had experienced great success with pineapple-centric designs. It was from that, the Fineapple, an absurd marriage of a mermaid and a pineapple, was born. Additional credit must be given to the Art Director I was working under at the time, Carter Watkins, for illustrating the palm tree and breeze-insinuating lines. You’ll notice two different iterations of the designs featured on the t-shirt mockups. There was a bit of disagreement on which was stronger. Ultimately, a final decision never had to be made. Although hat and t-shirt samples were made of the design, none of the products ever made it to bulk production. The entire Spring 19 collection, of which they were in, was scrapped as the Rowdy Gentleman business plan radically shifted.
