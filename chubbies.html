CHUBBIES

In January of 2020, I was brought aboard the design team of Chubbies - a brand that focuses primarily on men’s apparel- to help develop their Spring 2021 line. Chubbies describes itself as a “Weekend Apparel Brand,” with one of their most used catchphrases being “The weekend has arrived.” This is a guiding principle for the design of their products. They strive to have designs that are fun, enegertic, wild, loud, and celebratory. As a fellow weekend enthusiast, I was highly motivated to produce design work that exceed their standards. For inspiration, I looked a lot to an era that epitomized bright, loud, playful fashion; the 80’s. It was my goal to take this influence, update it, and add my own personal style to designs that sold like hot cakes. It’s with great pride that I’m able to write that these garments were sold in highly reputable retail stores across the country and sold out quickly via Chubbies’ ecommerce website.
